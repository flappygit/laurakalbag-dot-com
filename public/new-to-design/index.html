New To Design

Following my learning design post, I’ve started a little project called “new to design.” At the moment it just consists of a learn-design tag on my Pinboard bookmarks and a Twitter account @newtodesign so that I can share useful design resources (especially for those new to design.)

In the future, I hope it can be useful in other ways. Any suggestions or anything you want to see? You know how to get in touch…

A bit about me

Hello! I’m a designer from the UK, living in Sweden, and working as one half of Ind.ie. We make an app called Better that protects you while you’re browsing the web. My book Accessibility For Everyone is available in paperback and ebook from A Book Apart.
