SMACSS –; Scalable and Modular Architecture for CSS

Posted: 31 October 2012

I bought Jonathan Snook’s Scalable and Modular Architecture for CSS five months ago and, embarrassingly, I’ve only just read it. As a “flexible guide to developing sites small and large”, it’s fantastic. Last month I was lucky enough to see Jonathan speak about State-Based Design (a concept which he covers in SMACSS) at theFromTheFront conference and it reminded me to put the book at the top of my to-read pile. Read SMACSS –; Scalable and Modular Architecture for CSS.
