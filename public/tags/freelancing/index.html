  • Burnout

    Posted: 12 March 2013

    Burnout. Spending hours just staring into space when you’re supposed to be doing work, when you want to be doing work. Feeling foggy-headed and grey the whole time when you usually feel eager and excitable. Menial tasks are just about manageable, but anything requiring careful consideration or creative thinking feels impossible. I sleep but I still feel tired. I’m burned out. The last couple of months have really taken their toll. Read Burnout.
