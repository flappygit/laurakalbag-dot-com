The East Wing podcast

Posted: 6 February 2013

Yesterday I was a guest on Tim Smith‘s The East Wing podcast. It was a pre-recorded conversation and it was great fun. You can listen to it on the East Wing website, and it’ll be up on iTunes soon. We discussed how I got started in the industry (really sorry traditional Graphic Design people, I may have generalised a bit…), speaking at conferences, women in the industry and mentoring. As I said last night on Twitter, Tim Smith is such a great guy. Read The East Wing podcast.
